# Roadmap

What's next for VisvoAI, and why — kept public and current on purpose.

**[The board →](https://github.com/orgs/VisvoAI/projects/1)** shows the
live picture: what's actively being worked (Now), what's ready to pick up
(Planned), what's real but not urgent (Backlog), and what we've decided
*not* to do, with the reasoning stated (Out of scope, on purpose).

## Where this list came from

Every item traces back to an honest source, not a wishlist: a 70-problem
market audit that shopped these packages against real AI-integration pain
points developers hit today, plus gaps a documentation review found in our
own claims. Nothing here is speculative — each issue states the problem,
why it matters, a proposed direction (open to disagreement), and concrete
acceptance criteria.

## How to get involved

- **Pick up a `good-first-issue`** — these are scoped small and don't
  require deep familiarity with the codebase.
- **Have an idea that isn't here yet?** Start in
  [Discussions → Ideas](https://github.com/VisvoAI/visvoai/discussions/categories/ideas) —
  ideas that firm up graduate to issues.
- **See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md)** for how PRs land — reviewed
  here, applied upstream with authorship preserved.

## Labels

`tier-a` — small, contained, high-credibility work · `tier-b` — a real
feature, not urgent · `epic` — a multi-phase initiative · `area:*` — which
concern it touches · `package:*` — which package it touches.
